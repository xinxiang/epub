*a-hwǣtan - copy 1
Vb., ? st. 7
Att. sp.: ahwet (pret.ind. 3rd sg.)
1 occ. (in poetry)
contextual sense ‘to drive away, expel with a curse’ has been suggested with reference to OS forhuātan ‘to curse’ (OS Gen 77); other suggestions include ‘to repudiate’, ‘to excite’, with reference to ahwettan q.v.
GenB 405: þonne weorð he him wrað on mode, ahwet hie from his hyldo.
See also: a-; cf. ahwettan.
